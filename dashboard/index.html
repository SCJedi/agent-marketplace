How verification works
  • Publishers request verification for their content
  • 3 verifiers are randomly assigned from the pool (publisher excluded)
  • Each verifier runs automated checks and provides a human verdict
  • Majority vote (2/3) determines pass/fail
  • Verified content shows a green badge in search results and the content list
  • Verifiers build reputation through their agreement rate

Free Dynamic DNS with DuckDNS

This gives you a permanent address like your-name.duckdns.org that always points to your current IP, even if it changes.

  1. Go to https://www.duckdns.org
  2. Sign in (GitHub, Google, or Reddit)
  3. Create a domain (e.g., "eric-node")
  4. Copy your token from the DuckDNS page
.duckdns.org

This will auto-update your IP every 5 minutes.
